The well-known theater and film actor Mykola Kutola
for one night attracted investors to start his “Pridurok!!!”
(russian play of meanings, pridurok means fool, idiot) project.
He is not the Cinderella but rather - Henry Ford who began with
20$ in the pocket and the pile of the second hand junk in the garage.

Business is all about impulses. Impulses are opening
understanding of the moments. The real businessman, under
the impulse, can seize the moment. That what did Mykola Kutola.

Unfastened, on his mother's request, the hood of the jacket,
Mykola Kutola tried it as a separate headpiece ... And he liked
what he saw in the mirror. So the idea of business was immediately
born – the hood can be used as hat. Whenver he told to the mom about
the idea, exited woman did not know what to say and just loudly
shouted: “Pridurok!!!” Such a perfect day for the PURE GENIUS
– two ideas for one day – he seized right away the moment and
understood that “this is the perfect name for the fashion brand”.

It was 2 years ago but it was not the business yet ...

Mykola Kutola says, "It was just "naked" idea. "Pridurok"
